Provibe Podcast

Provibe podcast is a digital audio service transmitted via the internet.Podcasting on internet is usually reffered to as webcasting since it is not transmitted broadly through wireless means .

This podcast is completely discussing the problems faced by the campus students and the different artistic and intellectual activities of the students by the students for the students.

Podcasting platforms are now leveraging on internet devices and mobile apps.This goes to show that the podcasting platforms will continue to evolve due to the advancement of computer technology and software applications.
